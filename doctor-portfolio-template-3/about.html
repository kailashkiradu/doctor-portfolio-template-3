About Dr. Jeewan Kumar

Dr. Jeewan Mittal has to his credit over 10,000 cardiac intervention procedures. He is trained in performing complex cardiac procedures including emergency angioplasty or primary PTCA, multivessel angioplasty, treatment of chronic total occlusion, packer and CRDT device implantation.

He has over a decade of experience in treating complex heart conditions. His humane touch with use of cutting edge technologies helps ailing patients recover faster. Dr. Jeewan’s motto of care is “every patient is special and deserve the best care”. He is applying best scientific knowledge, technologies and passion to care to advance sciences in cardiovascular care.

Providing comprehensive and compassionate heart care for over a decade.
